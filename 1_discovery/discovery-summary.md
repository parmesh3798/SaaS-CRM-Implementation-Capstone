# Discovery Summary

## Overview
This document summarizes key findings from stakeholder discovery sessions conducted to understand sales, support, and leadership needs.

## Sales Team Needs
- Centralized view of all incoming leads
- Clear ownership of leads to avoid follow-up gaps
- Visibility into lead status and conversion progress
- Reduced manual tracking using spreadsheets

## Support Team Needs
- Single system to track all customer issues
- Clear case ownership and prioritization
- Ability to track response times and SLA adherence
- Historical view of customer issues

## Leadership Needs
- High-level visibility into sales pipeline health
- Insight into lead conversion rates
- Overview of support workload and SLA performance
- Data-driven decision making without manual reporting

## Key Success Criteria
- No lead goes unassigned or untracked
- Support cases meet defined SLAs
- Leadership dashboards reflect real-time data
